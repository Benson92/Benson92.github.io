Need to find a Hotel?

Though we are not booking any hotel blocks, we can give you some recommendations on where to stay in Oʻahu. Most of the hotels are located in Waikīkī and Ala Moana. This is probably the most convenient place to stay for most people. There are BNBs and beach houses scattered throughout the island. Some places are located in the countryside which can be a bit of a drive! Feel free to ask Leilani for her opinion before you book a place on how convenient it may or may not be for you.

Transportation

Wedding Day

We are happy to consider hiring a bus/van transport to and from Honolulu/Waikīkī if many of you choose to stay there. Please let Leilani know if you’d like to opt in for transport on the day. Otherwise, Haʻiku Gardens is about a twenty minute drive from Waikīkī. There are taxi services as well as Uber and Lyft.

General information

If you’re staying in Waikīkī, most touristy spots are quite walkable. There is also a trolley system that can get you around the town. However, if you do plan on going outside of Waikīkī (and we do recommend this), you should consider renting a car. If you have a valid driver’s license, you are legally able to drive in the state of Hawaiʻi. Just make sure you have your driver’s license with you when you do drive (and that you’re on the right side of the road).

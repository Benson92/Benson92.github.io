Events

4th January

Ceremony 10AM - 11AM

We are thrilled to invite you to join us as we celebrate our special day at the beautiful Haiku Gardens. Our journey of love deserves nothing less than a joyous, unforgettable celebration, and we believe this is the perfect place to share it with you. Their wedding planner has been working tirelessly to make sure every detail reflects our unique love story and ensure our day is one we'll cherish forever.

Reception11:30AM - 5PM

Our reception will be held at the award-winning Haleiwa Joes Seafood Grill, a beautiful setting overlooking the lush tropical landscape of Haiku Gardens. They're known for serving meals inspired by the beauty and bounty that surrounds them, and we can't wait for you to taste and enjoy it.

Cocktail Hour 7PM-8PM

We're excited to invite you to a fun-filled cocktail hour in the vibrant city of Honolulu, the details of which we'll be sharing soon. This gathering will allow us all to connect, catch up in a more casual setting. Picture sipping on tropical-inspired cocktails as the sun sets over the city, the warm Hawaiian breeze carrying laughter and good conversation. This will be a relaxed, joyful gathering, reflecting our journey together and the love and friendship that we share with each of you. We know that some of you will be joining us from afar, and we're thrilled to have this chance to share a slice of island life with you. Honolulu, with its vibrant culture and breathtaking scenery, offers a unique backdrop for this special event.
